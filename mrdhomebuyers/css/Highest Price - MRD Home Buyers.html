Benefits of Seller Financing

Benefit - HIGHEST PRICE

When selling a piece of real estate, the primary objective for most sellers is to put the most money in their pocket from the sale. To do this, people typically hire a realtor, prepare their property for the market, make a deal with a buyer from the open market, and close within a few months. While there’s nothing inherently wrong with this process, there are a lot of expenses eating away at your bottom line, both on the sell side and the buy side. Namely:

 

-Realtors (6% of sale price)

-Prepping house for sale ($1000+)

-Staging ($1000-$5000+)

-Appraisers ($500-$1000)

-Inspectors ($400-$800)

-Repair Addendums (1%-3%+ of sales price)

-Loan fees (2-4% for the buyer)

 

That’s a lot of mouths to feed! If you need to sell quickly, you skip a lot of those fees, but typically sell for a substantial discount to one of the many “We Buy Houses” investors, who low-ball you on price in exchange for a quick closing and fast cash. 

 

So what’s the answer? …Take installment payments! 

 

At MRD Homes, we pay you the highest price for your property. This price is often above the value indicated on sites like Zillow and Redfin. We’re able to do this by utilizing a simple principle: by delaying the rate at which you realize your profit from the sale of your property, we’re able to pay substantially more than you’d get from anyone else. We’re not house flippers. We renovate our assets, and rent them to high-quality tenants who enable us to make money using a buy-and-hold strategy that works by holding assets over long periods of time. 

 

In addition to paying the highest price, you’re typically not having to pay any single one of those aforementioned fees. Wouldn’t you prefer that money to go into your pocket? Check out these two examples of the same home selling for $400,000:

=$364,700 in your pocket

 

Sold to MRD home buyers - $410,000 ($10K higher!)

$41,000 today

+$138,000 (120 payments of $1150)

=$407,000 in your pocket


In addition to the more dollars in your pocket, there are numerous other benefits to selling to MRD Home Buyers including: becoming the bank, taking passive income for your property, zero hassle transactions, and even… saving on taxes.
