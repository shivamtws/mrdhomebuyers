What Is Seller Financing?

(Besides being the best way to sell your property?)

Seller financing is:

1.) Taking payments for your home over time, rather than a lump sum at closing.

2.) Having the buyer assume your existing loan, usually with an interest rate that is lower than today's rates. 

3.) A combination of both.

You become the bank for your buyer! After receiving a down payment, rest of the money is paid out over an agreed-upon time period. At the end of this term, any remaining balance is payable. Seller finance terms can be as short as 1 year to... as long as you'd like!

Why Do People Seller Finance Their House?

Couldn't Sell On-Market

Sometimes houses don't sell on market and it's no fault of the seller nor the house.

Cash Flow

Rising expenses and costs of property ownership make it increasingly difficult to make your property cash flow.

Landlord Headaches

Tenants and Property Managers can be a big source of headaches for property owners.

Passive Income Inheritance

Seller financing can lock in guaranteed cash flow for you and generations to come.

Tax Benefits

Seller financing can be the best way to limit your tax liability when selling your property.
