Why Choose MRD Home Buyers

Who We Are

MRD Home Buyers is an independently-owned real estate company specializing in residential and commercial properties nationwide. In 2004, our company was founded to provide a superior experience to sellers and investors alike by focusing on the transparency, professionalism, and service.

Our primary focus is the acquisition and management of residential properties. We achieve success by providing sellers with financing structures that are mutually advantageous to both parties in the transaction, creating win-win scenarios that provide long-term benefits to all. We are experts in our field and pride ourselves on our ability to explain and simplify concepts that may be unfamiliar to our clients.

We believe that selling your property off-market does not always mean selling for a substantial discount. We strive to provide the convenience and speed of an investor, the sale price of a retail buyer, and the integrity of a trusted business partner. Whether you're a property owner looking to sell, or an investor looking for passive returns, we invite you to contact us today.
