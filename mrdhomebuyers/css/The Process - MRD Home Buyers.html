The process to sell your house to MRD Home Buyers is as easy as it gets!

Our buying process is very similar to conventional sale, but with no open houses or hassle! There are 5 easy steps to selling your house to MRD Home Buyers and the entire process is completed in an average of 30 days.

 

See below for details on each step of the process:

1. Agree on Terms
2. Authorize Agreement
3. Title & Escrow
4. Inspections
5. Closing!
1. Agree on Terms

First, we reach an agreement in principle on the main key components to the sale: purchase price, financing structure, and closing date.

2. Authorize Agreement

This is where we make it real! We will electronically sign the agreement containing the price and terms we’ve agreed on for the sale of your property.

3. Title & Escrow

We open title and escrow. The title company will ensure that title is clear and able to be passed to a new buyer. They serve as an intermediary to the transaction and operate only under mutually agreed upon instructions.

4. Inspections

No need to worry here. Our team of professionals just takes a closer look at the property to ensure that all systems are in good working order.

We will never send you a list of minor issues found, or big lists of outrageous repairs, like is typical in other transactions.

5. Closing!

On or before our agreed-upon date, we close on the property and the title transfer is officially recorded with the county. It is on this date where we receive keys and legal title to the property.

Note - Closing dates are flexible and can be tailored to whatever length of time you desire. We can close escrow immediately following inspections or we can close on a date that is pre-chosen by you!
